General scroll bar container in hidden state

A general scroll bar displays a slider grip to indicate that the display data requires more space than is available.

  • When the cursor hovers over the vertical area under the slider grip, display a translucent background to indicate the slider track.
  • When the cursor hovers over the slider grip, change the slider grip color.
  • You may also hide the slider grip to reduce visual clutter on the UI, and then when the cursor hovers over a scrollable area, display the slider grip to indicate that there is more content to display. For example, you can use this interaction for a tree view component.
